STUDIOLAB ARCHITECTURE 2 COURSE: EXPLORE THE ARCHITECTURE OF AUDI TAN BINH SHOWROOM

On Wednesday (October 4), ISCM students had a field trip to the showroom of Audi Tan Binh. This is part of the StudioLab Architecture 2 course - a course in the course of Architecture and Urban Design inclusive Smart City program.

At the first 5S showroom in Vietnam of Audi, the students had the opportunity to directly observe and experience the distinctive design features of each area. Along with that, the trip also had the guidance and direct sharing from the leaders, experts, and lecturers in the profession.
