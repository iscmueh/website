The Executive Master program of Smart city and Innovation Management is where future leaders grab chances to improve their analytic and logical approach with solutions to smart, innovative and sustainable communities. The program also creates an international and connective environment, with international and national experts, lecturers as well as international opportunities to exchange with universities in Belgium (Hasselt University, and KU Leuven University), and Korea (Handong Global University). Completing the exchange programs, students will be considered to receive the 2nd degree degree from the abroading university.
