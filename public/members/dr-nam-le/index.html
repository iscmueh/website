Dr. Nam Le

Senior Lecturer

I am interested in applied mathematics, statistics, and operations research for optimal management and development of intervention strategies for buildings, infrastructure, and engineering systems.

SUMMARY OF ACTIVITIES/INTERESTS

  • Asset Management
  • Spatial Analysis
  • Operations Research

  • Project Management
