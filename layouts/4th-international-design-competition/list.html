Following the success of the 2023 International Design Competition, which focused on Bac Phuoc Thang - Vung Tau, the competition is officially returning with the theme (Re)engaging Cities’ Landscape for Cultural and Economic Vibrancy. This year, the selected site is in the Cai River area, aiming to propose innovative smart urban solutions that boost the local economy while preserving and enhancing the cityscape and coastal ecosystem of Nha Trang.
