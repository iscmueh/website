Design Studio Lab

Design Studio Lab is a platform for students, lecturers, professionals, and stakeholders to discuss and explore alternative urban development, restructuring, and preservation scenarios. Design is considered a “knowledge production machine.” That is, the interaction and design thinking in the project implementation process will test hypotheses, development scenarios, and new spatial concepts to propose alternative visions and development strategies for smart cities as well as apply technology for a more sustainable future. And of course, the results from the above quality thinking and design process can be considered a contribution of knowledge.
